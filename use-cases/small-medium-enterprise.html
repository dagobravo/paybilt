Small/Medium Enterprises are the backbone of the Canadian economy, representing 98% of all employer businesses in Canada and employing almost two-thirds of the country's total labour force (SOURCE: STAT CAN).

From salons and restaurants to auto service and retail stores - these businesses need preferred local payment methods to help keep up with the demands of day-to-day operations, keep employees happy, and keep customers coming back time and time again.

Give your business a competitive edge by providing customers with their preferred local payment methods. Paybilt will enhance your customers' payment experience, allowing you to accept payments quickly and easily, in whatever format your customer's desire.
